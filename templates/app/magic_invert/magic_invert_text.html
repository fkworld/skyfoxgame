"Magic Invert" is a cartoon style exciting invert puzzle game.

Easy to play, and pleasurable game for all ages.

To find the key circle and just invert circles's color in its direction and make all same color in a specified number of steps!

HOW TO PLAY

- Tap the circle and invert other circle's color by its direction.

- Try to fit them all the same color in limited steps.

- Don't worry! No time limits! And no need for wifi :)
